RANK BENEFITS

Benefits: Full benefits are made available to all members of The Galactic Order at the rank of Ensign. Novitiates receive only partial benefits...

Novitiate Benefits:
  • Armor
  • Tools
  • Housing
  • Limited Healthcare

All Benefits Provided:
  • Armor
  • Weapons
  • Ammo
  • Tools
  • Food/Supplies
  • Housing
  • Financial Loans
  • Healthcare
  • Vision Care
  • Dental
  • Unlimited PTO (Paid Time Off)

Unit Salaries:
  • 0 / Day
  • 100 / Day
  • 250 / Day
  • 500 / Day

Pay Dates: Unit Salary is paid bi-weekly; every other Sunday.

Veteran Bonuses: Obtained through roadmap goals, each Veteran Bonus gives +10% to Daily Salary.

Members above Rank 0: Novitiate may apply for aUEC Loans. Depending on the individual’s rank, determines the limit of the loan they may receive. There is No pay back date, however, interest rates will continue to climb over time and penalties are applied for members with active loans.

Unit Loan Limits
  • Rank 1: - 750,000 aUEC Limit
  • Rank 2: - 3,000,000 aUEC Limit
  • Rank 3: - 7,500,000 aUEC Limit

Loan Applications must be submitted to an officer and may only be approved by rank 4 leadership or higher. Loans are typically pulled from Battalion funding so there may be limits based on battalion type and size as funds may need to be reserved for other purposes. Refusal of loans may be given with or without explanation.

Interest Rates:
  • 1st Week: 5% on Payments.
  • After 1 Week: 5% changes to 15% on Payments.
  • After 1 Month: +10% added on Payments (total: 25%).
  • Each additional Month will add +10% interest.
  • Promotions and Salary are withheld while loans are active.

Note: Initial (in-game) 0.5% loan Transfer fees are not factored into the Loan Limit listed above but will be added to the total owed, and must be paid in addition to interest rates.

Warning: Members who take out a loan and then leave the org will be permanently banned from re-entering until the loan is paid.

Loan Payments:

Payments can be made at any time, and for any amount (minimum: 100,000 aUEC), but must be made directly to the org bank or a member of leadership while both parties are online (in-game) to witness the transaction. Interest rates are applied based on the payment amount and the accrued percentage (%), which is deducted from the payment’s total, along with transfer fees as these must be covered by the loan recipient.

Exam Details

Test Applicants will have 30 Minutes to answer 50 Multiple Choice Questions and must score 70% or higher in order to Pass. All Questions & Answers are related to Galactic Order content with a focus on Rules, Laws & Protocols (found on the org info site).

The Exam will appear and the timer will begin once the applicant presses the “Start Exam” button, which appears after selecting one's Battalion & Name. Closing or refreshing the window / tab, at any point, will immediately end the exam. The exam will also end when the applicant either runs out of time or clicks the “Submit” button. All answers that are not completed within the time-limit will be marked as incorrect.

Note 1: Some questions will require selecting multiple listed answers to be counted as correct.

Note 2: Only units whom have not yet passed the exam will appear in the dropdown list provided.

After the exam ends, the results will calculate and appear below as a Pass or Fail.

It is worth noting, by no means do we expect members to know everything about Star Citizen or the Org. Even slight familiarity with our content and the ability to quickly access any information is almost more valuable than simply knowing, as the content tends to change and update regularly. For this reason, the Exam is completely open source with no restriction to notes, web-pages, tabs or available information for the applicant as they answer each question.
