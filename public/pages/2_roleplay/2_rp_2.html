OFFICER INFORMATION

Officer Rank 3:

    The term “Officer” refers to a unit of rank 3 or higher. With this title, a Galactic Order member gains new responsibilities, permissions and authority within the chain of command. This is a List of every added permission for Rank 3:

  1. Gains Warden & Preceptor roles on the RSI Org site.
    • Warden: Manage Members, Ranks & Roles.
    • Preceptor: Manage Applicants.

  2. Gains Official Galactic Order information site Sign-In Username & Password.
    • Temporarily, this will be a static username and password. Eventually, once website security is improved, Officers will have the option to Sign-Up independently, granting unique access privileges based on Division.

  3. Report Warnings, Patrols, Hours & Missions on the Galactic Order Roster.
    • Visit Full Roster page.
    • Select the Button on the Left, revealing additional options.
    • Select File Report, then Battalion & Unit Name.
    • When all info is filled out, press Submit to apply changes.

  4. Report accepted Applicants on the Galactic Order Roster
    • Visit Full Roster page.
    • Select the Button on the Left, revealing additional options.
    • Select Register Unit and proceed to fill all information...
      1. Star Citizen Name
      2. Discord Profile
      3. Recruiter's Name(optional)
      4. Starting Battalion
      5. Start Date
    • When all info is filled out, press Submit to apply changes.

    Important Note: If Discord Profile info can't be provided, leave Starting Battalion Empty.

  5. Manage Roles in Discord
    • Promotions and Rank changes may be made only after 1st Officer or Captain reviews, implements & closes comments.
    • Same goes for CIG Org promotions and Rank Changes.

  6. Mute, Deafen and Move Members in Discord channels.
  7. Privaleges to Post in Events and Announcement channels.
  8. Priority Speak in RP Discord channels

Note: Officers must be familiar with:

  • Rules & Laws
  • Protocol

The Title of Officer is one of great value and importance to The Galactic Order. Currently, there are 3 different ranks of Officer, each with varying responsabilities and duties.

Rank 3 Officers (Commanders) directly oversee same-division members of their Battalion. They organize their units, record/report the progression based on given assignments and may also promote up to Rank 2. This includes completed Patrols, Hours, and Missions. Commanders may also organize Events and make Announcements on Discord, usually with a focus on division-related tasks.

  • More information can be found on Rank Information page of The Galactic Order Info site.

Rank 3.5 Officers (1st Officers) are assigned by Captains whom wish to delegate responsabilities to someone they trust. These typically include distribution of Unit Salary Payments, recording # of Events and # of Recruits for all Battalion units, and approving reports submitted by Commanders on the Roster. 1st Officers may also organize Events, make Announcements on Discord and promote members up to Rank 3, pending Captain's approval.

  • More information can be found on Rank Information page of The Galactic Order Info site.

Rank 4 Officers (Captains) are entirely responsaible for reporting to Admiralty and managing their Battalion. They must also organize / handle Battalion funds which can drastically vary in complexity based on the battalion type and how many units it contains. Captains are also obligated to maintain constant availability to all members they oversee. This requires a comprehension of all Galactic Order knowledge and policies to handle any situation they may find themselves in. In terms of duty, Captains shoulder the longest list of responsabilities, even with an assigned 1st Officer.

  • More information can be found on Captain Info page of The Galactic Order Info site.

The Title of Acting Officer is slightly different from the usual Rank 3 Officer positions. Typically, an individual becomes an Officer through the standard means of rank progression, based on activity and determination that is expressed through their achievements recorded in The Galactic Order Roster. However, in times of need, when there are limited members of authority to accommodate a large gap in the ranks, experienced individuals will receive the Leadership Commendation by a Captain or Admiral, with the available option to hold the temporary title of Acting Officer. This is a premature promotion to Rank 3, often offered to Rank 2 units of that Division who have shared interest in the advancements of The Galactic Order along with ambition for holding greater authority & responsibility.

Acting Officers receive all benefits and advantages of the rank they are given. For all intended purposes, they are Rank 3 in their Division. However, this role comes with some limitations that should be noted…

Temporary:
  • As it is a temporary position, should a subordinate of the same Battalion and Division rise through the ranks, surpassing their Acting Officer in prerequisite qualifications, said Officer’s position may be at risk for replacement by a now, more qualified, candidate. For this reason, it is recommended that Acting Officers make their best efforts to earn the position they’ve been given, removing the “Acting” title and granting official permanence in the Role.

Division Change:
  • Because it is not a position gained through standard means, an Acting Officer must maintain the role until they’ve either been replaced by another, or reached the necessary minimum prerequisites for their position. If replaced, their time served as an Acting Officer will not be counted towards Leadership progression.

Leadership Progression:
  • Promotion to Acting Officer does not count towards further advancements in rank progression for leadership. They cannot be promoted to 1st Officer and their Officer status won’t be counted for progression to Rank 4 Captain, until they’ve reached the necessary minimum prerequisites for their position.

Activity:
  • As the name implies, Acting Officers must maintain some level of activity in order to continue serving in the role they’ve been assigned. Organizing, hosting and/or participating in events & org activities is the preferred level of activity, however, involvement within the Discord would also suffice. This activity must be observed by the Acting Officer’s Captain, or Admiral, at least once every 2 weeks. If inactivity is noted to occur, the Acting Officer will be relieved of their position.

Important Note: It is critical to state that, although assignment to Acting Officer comes with some restraints, it is a position of incredible honor. It is not only proof of the individual's believed qualifications but also proof of Leadership’s trust & confidence in that individual’s capabilities. Appointment of this role is done rarely but never lightly.
