CONTACT US

If you are interested in Joining The Galactic Order, feel free to visit our Discord and our RSI Org page!

Also feel free to check out our instagram!

We of The Galactic Order Leadership Division do our best to maintain availability for any questions, concerns or suggestions our members may have. The best means for communication, at this time, is through our Discord. Feel free to reach out to any Admirals, Captains, 1st Officers or Commanders there. Also feel free to ask any questions you may have in our public chats. Depending on your Discord role, some group chats may not be visible but the General chat is available to everyone visiting the discord who has accepted our Rules & Laws in the intro channels.

If you've not yet visited our Discord, feel free to hop in any time and chill with us. We are a very warm and welcoming group of gamers who love meeting new people.

Applications:

Applications sent to the RSI Org site have no requirements, although we do recommend a mention to how you found the org or who recruited you as this info not only helps us to further improve our recruitment methods but recruit numbers also aid in rank progression.

Some members also use the application section to give a brief roleplay character backstory/description, but this is completely optional.
