RANK UNKNOWN: CONSCRIPT

Acting much like a contracted mercenary, Conscripts operate outside the official ranks of The Galactic Order military. They have no restrictions to the rules and regulations with regards to attire, responsibilities and command. However, this also means they hold no authority in the chain of command and receive no benefits or unit allowances. Conscripts are often hired under a general contract with very specific parameters based on a percentage of funds earned upon completion of assisted missions and tasks.

RANK 0: NOVITIATE

Novitiate is the starting Rank within The Galactic Order military. However, they have a very unique role with nearly full access to the tasks and opportunities available to rank 1 units, with exceptions to Equipment, Authority and Benefits. A Novitiate may work as a Trooper, Operator or Medic (within reason) to determine their preferred future branch. Upon their 1st promotion to Rank 1, a Novitiate may choose between the 3 available Divisions (Combat, Engineering or Medical). Although a division choices can be changed, it is viewed as a long-term commitment of duty. For this reason, it is recommended that each Novitiate study the roles and responsibilities of each option before making a decision.

RANK 1: ENSIGN TROOPER

Often simply referred to as just “Trooper”, these act primarily as foot-soldiers, guards and secondary turret operators within a Battalion. Troopers make up a majority of The Galactic Order’s military and are vital to maintaining peace, stability and law throughout the Galaxy. Sadly, due to their large range of combat-associated responsabilities, they are the highest risk within a battalion, holding the greatest mortality rate of any other position.

    RANK 3: TROOPER COMMANDER

    Commanders are the head of their division within a Battalion, reporting only to the Captain and 1st Officer, to maintain the chain of command. However, Commanders may adopt the roles and responsibilities of other units in their division should they so choose or in times of desperation.

    RANK 2: OUTRIDER

    These units operate within Special Operations sub-division branch of the Combat Division. They are used primarily for: solo or group scouting parties, providing support in large scale assaults and performing stealth / infiltration maneuvers. They are found operating stealth fighters, hacking vehicles, hover vehicles deployed from drop ships mid-atmosphere, and among ground assault troops (usually in a sniper or hacker role). Due to their covert nature, all completed hours and missions are kept off-book from official documents and rosters, inhibiting their chances for promotion outside their sub-division. They also hold no authority within the chain of command.

    RANK 3: AGENT

    Agents are high-priority covert units of the Special Operations sub-division branch of the Combat Division. They operate completely off-grid, existing purely within the shadows, unregistered to battalions or rosters and with their identities known only to the highest ranked members of the Leadership division.

    They specialize in deep-cover espionage, infiltration and assassination operations, often on assignment for long periods of time to eliminate political leaders and destabilize government bodies. They’ve also been rumored for use to eliminate corrupt members within the Galactic Order itself. In many instances, they will be planted within divisions as a different rank, either under orders or to simply occupy their down-time between assignments. Progression to this role is classified along with further details pertaining to the position.

    RANK 1: ENSIGN MEDIC

    Often referred to as “Field Medic” or just “Medic”, these units provide Tier 3 emergency response care and patient recovery/transport for sever injuries, often to Troopers in ground combat. Unlike the medical units of most military bodies, these units are not upheld by non-combatant oaths, and may provide lethal support from behind the front lines of battle. Medics are typically found on drop ships, within Trooper squads, assisting in medical bays or on-board patient recovery vessels. They have a slightly lower mortality rate than that of Ensgin Trooper.

    RANK 4: ELITE GUARD

    These units operate within the Elite sub-division branch of the Leadership Division. They are an alternate position to Rank 4: Captain, serving as top-tier protective detail to Admirals of the leadership division and other high priority members within the Galactic Order. Their soul duty is the protection and execution of their Admiral’s will and overall vision, often acting as an extension of the leader they serve. They hold no independent voice in the decisions of Leadership and zero Command authority over others. However, they take orders only from their assigned Admiral and have unrestricted access to nearly all confidential information, territory locations, division documentations and the inner workings of the organization, limited only by what Leadership deems necessary.

    RANK 3.5: 1ST OFFICER

    This role is one highly respected within any battalion. They are appointed only by the Battalion’s acting Captain and often promoted from the position of Commander. Their Duty is to adopt some of the Captain’s many responsibilities such as recording Roster progression and Unit Allowance distribution, while also delegating to other Officers, specifically Commanders. 1st Officers are essentially viewed as Captains in training.

    RANK 4: CAPTAIN

    More commonly found aboard larger ships and vessels, Captains are held to the highest of standards, with nearly complete authority and command over their own personal battalion which may consist of multiple ships, up to 25 crew and specific roles, all of which the captain claims full responsibility. In the absence of an Admiral, the captain’s word is law first and foremost followed by their appointed 1st officer, typically promoted from the position of Commander, acting outside any division. This information can all vary depending on the battalion type and its size along with any limits declared by the assigned fleet Admiralty.

    The Rank of Captain is awarded in 1 of two situations. Either a Captain is promoted to assume command of an existing Battalion or they are appointed to form their own. Both are considered a great honor and hold unique challenges.
