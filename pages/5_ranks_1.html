CONSCRIPT

Acting much like a contracted mercenary, Conscripts operate outside the official ranks of The Galactic Order military. They have no restrictions to the rules and regulations with regards to attire, responsibilities and command. However, this also means they hold no authority in the chain of command and receive no benefits or unit allowances. Conscripts are often hired under a general contract with very specific parameters based on a percentage of funds earned upon completion of assisted missions and tasks.

NOVITIATE

As entry level, Novitiate is the lowest rank to be found within a Battalion. They hold a very unique placement as they are below every rank, no matter the division, but also have nearly full access to the responsibilities and opportunities available to every division’s Rank 1 position with the exceptions being Equipment, Authority and Benefits. A Novitiate may work as a Trooper, Operator or Medic (within reason) as a means to determine their preferred branch. They may even rotate these responsibilities at will but only with the approval of a superior officer of rank 2 or higher. When presented with their first promotion, often after a performance review following service to one or multiple divisions, a Novitiate will have their pick of Division. Though this decision can be changed at later dates, this is often considered a long-term commitment of duty. For this reason, it is recommended, though not required, that each Novitiate study the roles and responsibilities of each division before deciding.

ENSIGN TROOPER

Often simply referred to as just “Trooper”, these act primarily as foot-soldiers, guards and secondary turret operators within a Battalion. Troopers make up a majority of The Galactic Order’s military and are vital to maintaining peace, stability and law throughout the Galaxy. Sadly, due to their large range of combat-associated responsabilities, they are the highest risk within a battalion, holding the greatest mortality rate of any other position.

TROOPER COMMANDER

Commanders are the head of their division within a Battalion, reporting only to the Captain and 1st Officer, to maintain the chain of command. Trooper Commanders are more often found on the bridge of a ship or various strategy rooms, furthest from the threat of battle, as the role of Commander is a significant one that holds greater value providing instructions based on their extensive knowledge and experience. However, Commanders may adopt the roles and responsibilities of other units in their division should they so choose or in times of desperation.

OUTRIDER

These units operate within Special Operations sub-division branch of the Combat Division. They are used primarily for: solo or group scouting parties, providing support in large scale assaults and performing stealth / infiltration maneuvers. They are found operating stealth fighters, hacking vehicles, hover vehicles deployed from drop ships mid-atmosphere, and among ground assault troops (usually in a sniper or hacker role). Due to their covert nature, all completed hours and missions are kept off-book from official documents and rosters, inhibiting their chances or promotion outside their sub-division. They also hold no authority within the chain of command.

These units are the lowest level of the Engineering Division. They are mostly seen as pilots for light fighters, small transports and small to medium cargo ships. When outside a cockpit, they are frequently found working cargo bays, engineering sections or operating various terminals on-board a ship. This role tends to be more laborious outside of battle than other divisions but they hold the lowest mortality rate and largest array of tools and tasks available.

ENSIGN MEDIC

Often referred to as “Field Medic” or just “Medic”, these units provide Tier 3 emergency response care and patient recovery/transport for sever injuries, often to Troopers in ground combat. Unlike the medical units of most military bodies, these units are not upheld by non-combatant oaths, and may provide lethal support from behind the front lines of battle. Medics are typically found on drop ships, within Trooper squads, assisting in medical bays or on-board patient recovery vessels. They have a slightly lower mortality rate than that of Ensgin Trooper.
