RANK BENEFITS

Benefits: Full benefits are made available to all members of The Galactic Order at the rank of Ensign. Novitiates receive only partial benefits...

Novitiate Benefits:
  • Armor
  • Tools
  • Housing
  • Limited Healthcare

All Benefits Provided:
  • Armor
  • Weapons
  • Ammo
  • Tools
  • Food/Supplies
  • Housing
  • Financial Loans
  • Healthcare
  • Vision Care
  • Dental
  • Unlimited PTO (Paid Time Off)

Unit Salaries:
  • 0 / Day
  • 100 / Day
  • 250 / Day
  • 500 / Day

Pay Dates: Unit Salary is paid bi-weekly; every other Sunday.

Veteran Bonuses: Obtained through roadmap goals, each Veteran Bonus gives +10% to Daily Salary.

Members above Rank 0: Novitiate may apply for aUEC Loans. Depending on the individual’s rank, determines the limit of the loan they may receive and also determines the amount of time they will be given before the loan must be repaid.

Loans that exceed the initial pay-back date will issue a 1st strike warning and adds a +10% interest fee from the total amount loaned with a 3 months extension. Failure to pay-back the full loan after the 2nd date will result in a 2nd warning with an additional +10% interest fee from the total amount initially loaned with a 1 month extension. Failure to pay the full amount after the 2nd extension will result in either demotion with a temporary rank progression ban or termination depending on the individual’s attempts at payment return.

Unit Loan Limits
  • Rank 1: - 500,000 aUEC Limit / 1 Month
  • Rank 2: - 2,500,000 aUEC Limit / 2 Months
  • Rank 3: - 5,000,000 aUEC Limit / 5 Months

Loan Applications must be submitted to an officer and may only be approved by rank 4 leadership or higher. Loans are typically pulled from Battalion funding so there may be limits based on battalion type and size as funds may need to be reserved for other purposes. Refusal of loans may be given with or without explanation.

Note: Initial loan transfer fees are not factored into the Loan Limit listed above. However, these fees are added into the total amount owed. Transfer fees (in-game) are +5% of the total amount sent and are not issued by the Galactic Order. All current loans and information can be viewed in the Battalion Roster.
