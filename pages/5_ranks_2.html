RANK BENEFITS

Benefits: Full benefits are made available to all members of The Galactic Order at the rank of Ensign. Novitiates receive only partial benefits...

Novitiate Benefits:
  • Armor
  • Tools
  • Housing
  • Limited Healthcare

All Benefits Provided:
  • Armor
  • Weapons
  • Ammo
  • Tools
  • Food/Supplies
  • Housing
  • Financial Loans
  • Healthcare
  • Vision Care
  • Dental
  • Unlimited PTO (Paid Time Off)

Unit Salaries:
  • 0 / Day
  • 100 / Day
  • 250 / Day
  • 500 / Day

Pay Dates: Unit Salary is paid bi-weekly; every other Sunday.

Veteran Bonuses: Obtained through roadmap goals, each Veteran Bonus gives +10% to Daily Salary.

Members above Rank 0: Novitiate may apply for aUEC Loans. Depending on the individualâ€™s rank, determines the limit of the loan they may receive. There is No pay back date, however, interest rates will continue to climb over time and penalties are applied for members with active loans.

Unit Loan Limits
  • Rank 1: - 750,000 aUEC Limit
  • Rank 2: - 3,000,000 aUEC Limit
  • Rank 3: - 7,500,000 aUEC Limit

Loan Applications must be submitted to an officer and may only be approved by rank 4 leadership or higher. Loans are typically pulled from Battalion funding so there may be limits based on battalion type and size as funds may need to be reserved for other purposes. Refusal of loans may be given with or without explanation.

Interest Rates:
  • 1st Week: 5% on Returns.
  • After 1 Week: 5% changes to 15% on Returns.
  • After 1 Month: +10% added on Returns (total: 25%).
  • Each additional Month will add +10% interest.
  • Promotions and Salary are withheld while loans are active.

Note: Initial (in-game) 0.5% loan Transfer fees are not factored into the Loan Limit listed above but will be added to the total owed, and must be paid in addition to interest rates.

Warning: Members who take out a loan and then leave the org will be permanently banned from re-entering until the loan is paid.

Loan Payments:

Payments can be made at any time, and for any amount, but must be made directly to the org bank or a member of leadership while both parties are online (in-game) to witness the transaction.
