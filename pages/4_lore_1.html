CLASSIFICATIONS

A Division refers to the field of study or military branch within the Galactic Order that each individual unit may fall under. Currently there are 5 main Divisions:

  1. Leadership Division: This division is comprised of The Galactic Order's highest-ranking members of authority. They are the final voice on all matters within the org and primarily consist of:
    2. 1st Officers
    Captains
    Vice-Admirals
    Admirals

  2. Combat Division: This division is focused on military and police action. It involves security, protection and maintaining peace through the enforcement of laws, rules and policy within The Galactic Order’s territory. This is actually the largest division, consisting primarily of:
    3. Troopers
    Lieutenant-Troopers
    Shock Troopers
    Trooper-Commanders

  3. Engineering Division: This division is likely the most widely spread in terms of responsibilities and scope. It consists of everything from the gathering, refining and organizing of resources to the maintenance, production and operation of most (if not all) ships, vehicles and structures within the Galactic Order. This includes (but is not limited to):
    4. Miners Craftsmen
    Salvagers Pilots
    Cargo Delivery Co-Pilots
    Cargo-bay Workers Operators
    Tradesmen Maintenance

  4. Medical Division: This division holds two primary purposes: The health and well-being of all within the galactic Order, and The modifying and progression of the human race with the implementation of scientific discoveries to further execute the evolution of all life. This division consists of medical professionals who prioritize the health and progression of Galactic Order members. These roles mostly include:
    5. Field Medic
    Lieutenant-Corpsman
    Medical-Commander

  5. Science Division: The science division works very closely with the engineering and medical division, with a primary focus on progressing human evolution as well as technological advancements to greater strengthen the Galactic Order’s power and presence throughout the vers. Though very early in its development, the science division is miles ahead nearly every other organization and they grow in numbers and knowledge with every passing day. Those of this division are more commonly found within designated labs or aboard exploratory vessels conducting research and making new discoveries.
    6. Field Researcher
    Labratory Technologist
    Chief Science-Officer

These exist within only but a few divisions as alternate promotion branches available to some units as a possible variant to the standard path of progression. These are often slightly easier to achieve in terms of prerequisites to what would normally be expected from a role of the same rank but also hold a number of disadvantages such as hindering rank progression beyond the sub-division role or being excluded from the standard chain of command giving less authority within the Galactic Order. It is worth noting that sub-divisions occasionally act as a hybrid, incorporating privileges and responsibilities from other division roles as well as prerequisites. In many cases, these specialized roles will be made available to those whom have served in multiple divisions.

  1. Special Operations: This sub-division, also referred to as Special Ops or Spec ops, is a branch of the Combat Division, with relative ties and similarities to Engineering. It consists of two primary Units (Outrider & Agent) that each utilize stealth, infiltration and intelligence gathering, to name only but a few, in order to accomplish specialized assignments/missions. Members of this division hold no authority within the Galactic order chain of command and their records of service are redacted or temporarily frozen.

  2. Elite: This sub-division is a branch of the Leadership Division, though it is important to distinguish that units of this sub-division hold no official authority within the chain of command apart from what is given by members of Leadership. Elite sub-division units are at the top of their field; individuals whom mostly operate as they see fit without the complications and responsibilities of command. They answer only to the high-ranking members of the Leadership Division unless otherwise stated in their unit card description.

Primary Ships: Each battalion is run by a Captain and is typically centered around a primary ship. These ships must be larger than 90 meters in length with a large, sub-capitol or capitol classification.

Primary ships may serve one function or multiple but must fall under a Type classification to determine funding and their role within the Fleet.

Units: Most battalions utilize units from multiple divisions to function at or above 75% capability, requiring 5 or more crew members minimum to operate (not factoring in different shifts). The standard battalion size is anywhere between 10 to 25 units when taking shifts and leadership into consideration. More than 25 units will classify the battalion as Large, a title reserved typically for special battalion types such as Admiralty or Academy.

Fighters & Support: For additional ships within a battalion, the primary ship must meet the minimum safety requirements such as docking or a hanger to provide safe crew transfers to accommodate shift changes and crew demands.

Statistically speaking, if all regulations and minimum requirements are met, there is technically no max number of ships that may be present within a single battalion, the exception being what the Battalion Captain is capable / comfortable handling and limits set by Admiralty.

    • There are several Battalion Types, each organized to perform specific tasks and hold focused responsibilities based on the chosen primary Ship. Battalions have their own unique blend of units from each division, assigned to accommodate the necessary roles required for optimal efficiency. Listed below are the battalion types currently implemented within The Galactic Order...

    1. Admiralty: Led by an Admiral, this Battalion’s Primary Ship is considered the “Flag Ship”, acting as the hub for all decisions within the Fleet. The most common Primary Ships for this type are multi-purpose.

    2. Academy: This type has the expressed and specific purpose of research, development, education, and unit training. The majority of The Galactic Order’s overall progression occurs in these battalions as they are often the starting point for nearly all Novitiates first entering the Organization. As Academy Battalions lead into all other Battalion types, units from every Division can be found here. Similar to Admiralty, these often utilize a multi-purpose Primary Ship along with many support ships and fighters, giving the nickname: “Mini-Fleets”.

    3. Battle: Although combat units are in every battalion, this type is often centered around protection, preservation of peace and establishing law & order with militant force. The most common Primary Ships for this type are Carriers, Gun Ships, Frigates & Corvettes.

    4. Industrial: This Battalion is mostly comprised of engineering division units and holds the largest array of career positions and responsibilities. The most common Primary Ships for this type are heavy: Salvage, Mining, Transport/Touring, Freight, Repair, Refueling & Construction.

    5. Medivac: This Battalion mostly consists of medical division units and has a primary focus on Search and Rescue. The most common Primary Ship for this type is the Endeavor with Hospital Attachments.

    6. Exploration: This battalion mostly consists of science division units and leads in discovery, colonization and scientific progression. The most common Primary Ships for this type are Expedition, Touring & Science.

    Battalions are named with a designation number (F-##) to indicate the assigned Fleet/Admiral followed by the Captain’s Last name, the first Letter of their battalion Type (B for Battle, E for Exploration, etc…) and a number starting at ‘01’. This number is significant for fleets with multiple Battalions of the same type.

      Example:
    • F-01 Remington B-01
    • Fleet #1
    • Captain Remington
    • Battle battalion #1
