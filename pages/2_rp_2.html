OFFICER INFORMATION

Officer Rank 3:

    The term “Officer” refers to a unit of rank 3 or higher. With this title, a Galactic Order member gains new responsibilities, permissions and authority within the chain of command. This is a List of every added permission based on Officer rank:

  1. Gains Warden & Preceptor roles on CIG Org site.
    • Warden: Manage Members, Ranks & Roles.
    • Preceptor: Manage Applicants.

  2. Report Warnings, Patrols, Hours & Missions on Galactic Order Roster (as Comments).
    • Visit Members page.
    • Right Click on Member’s Name Box.
    • Select Comment and write Warnings, Hours & Missions for that member.

    Note: This system will change one Roster is complete on Galactic Order Info site.

    Example:
    • Hours: 1
    • Turret: 5
    • Warning: Uniform/Armor reminder

  3. Report accepted Applicants (as Comments)
    • Visit Members page.
    • Right Click on Empty Name Box
    • Select Comment and write Member’s in-game name and Discord Profile Name (not Nickname).
    Example:
    • Name: TheBeardedPIckle
    • Discord Profile: BeardedPickle#8689

  4. Promotions in Roster
    • Write Comment in Rank and/or Division boxes
    • Write Comment in Last Date Promoted box

    Note: Comments are closed once implemented.

  5. Manage Roles in Discord
    • Promotions and Rank changes may be made only after 1st Officer or Captain reviews, implements & closes comments.
    • Same goes for CIG Org promotions and Rank Changes.

  6. Mute, Deafen and Move Members in Discord channels.
  7. Privaleges to Post in Events and Announcement channels.
  8. Priority Speak in RP Discord channels

Note: Officers must be familiar with:

  • Rules & Laws
  • Protocol
  • Classifications
  • Combat Theory
  • Rank Informtation
  • Rank Progression

The Title of Officer is one of great value and importance to The Galactic Order. Currently, there are 3 different ranks of Officer, each with varying responsabilities and duties.

Rank 3 Officers (Commanders) directly oversee same-division members of their Battalion. They organize their units, record/report the progression based on given assignments and may also promote up to Rank 2. This includes completed Patrols, Hours, and Missions. Commanders may also organize Events and make Announcements on Discord, usually with a focus on division-related tasks.

  • More informtation can be found on Rank Informtation page of The Galactic Order Info site.

Rank 3.5 Officers (1st Officers) are assigned by Captains whom wish to delegate responsabilities to someone they trust. These typically include distribution of Unit Allowances, recording # of Events and # of Recruits for all Battalion units, and approving reports submitted by Commanders on the Roster. 1st Officers may also organize Events, make Announcements on Discord and promote members up to Rank 3, pending Captain's approval.

  • More informtation can be found on Rank Informtation page of The Galactic Order Info site.

Rank 4 Officers (Captains) are entirely responsaible for reporting to Admiralty and managing their Battalion. They must also organize / handle Battalion funds which can drastically varry in complexity based on the battalion type and how many units it contains. Captains are also obligated to maintain constant availability to all members they oversee. This requires a comprehension of all Galactic Order knowledge and policies to handle any situation they may find themselves in. In terms of duty, Captains shoulder the longest list of responsabilities, even with an assigned 1st Officer.

  • More information can be found on Captain Info page of The Galactic Order Info site.
