INTRODUCTION

First, would love to offer a BIG WELCOME to all visitors on The Galactic Order Info site! As the name suggests, this is an informational site built to provide members, potential applicants and allies, all available details about The Galactic Order. To start, here is a brief introduction about our organization:

Closely resembling the Empire from Star Wars, Starfleet from Star Trek, or the Imperium of Man from Warhammer 40,000 Universe, The Galactic Order is a Roleplaying organization within the Star Citizen universe. At the gameâ€™s current alpha development state, our org has mostly been focused on creating and refining our overall structure while gathering in numbers for when Star Citizen is eventually fleshed out further to incorporate gameplay elements that can continue the progression of our goals.

We take great pride in the warm community we have formed and are consistently welcoming to all whom wish to join our ranks or help nurture alliances, so long as there are no conflicts in RP ideology. On the subject of RP, we are a Roleplaying org, which means there are a few rules, laws and protocols required of our members to follow when joining the chain of command. The rules & laws, specifically, are quite simple and can be found listed below.

RULES & LAWS

  • Don't be a jerk or troll
  • Show respect to others regardless of Race, Religion, Culture, Political Beliefs, Sexual Orientation or Gender. We will not tolerate hate speech or discrimination.
  • With any Conflicts, speak to a superior on Discord of the situation (no matter how big or small).
  • No uploading of explicit content to the Discord, which includes nudity, gore, or cruelty towards humans or animals.
  • Please avoid discussing real world politics or religion in the org Discord. This org is for gaming and fun so we recommend private messages for such topics.

These will be expanded on a later date when deemed necessary. These Laws mostly focus on behavior within the Discord where most communication takes place. Links to the Discord can be found on the RSI Org page and in Contact Us.

  • Wear the attire of your current rank only. Details located in Ranks Informtaion.
  • Follow the chain of command and RolePlay Protocols given in Protocol.
  • Do not perform duties outside those of your rank without permission from a superior officer of Rank 3 or higher.
  • Do not attack innocent civilians (other players), or cause damage to non-hostile targets. We are against griefing.
  • Engaging pirates (NPCs or Wanted Players) that would harm you, or citizens, is allowed.
  • To greatly help with communication, we also require that new members change their Discord server nickname to reflect their in-game character names.

Violation of these rules will result in a warning before demotion. Further violations will lead to termination.

Rank and organization related information is provided on The Galactic Order Info site. Keep in mind that these rules apply only in-game and only when associating with other Galactic Order members, activities or events.
